Home Automation with Alexa and Google Home

From this project we have tried to integrate the normal devices like fan, light, charging socket etc with voice or Android app.

Hardware Components used were NodeMCU , Arduino, etc and software used were DialogFlow, Firebase, Android Studio for the app etc.
